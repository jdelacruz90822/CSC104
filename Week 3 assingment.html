The history of Doughnuts

Doughnuts evolved from ancient fried-dough traditions and Dutch settlers' olykeoks (oil cakes) to the modern treat,

with the ring shape and hole added in the mid-19th century.

"Doughnut Girls"

Doughnuts gained significant popularity in the United states.

During World War I, they served them to soldiers, this trend was

than popularized further by the invention of the first automatic

doughnut-making machine in 1920!

! Fun Facts about doughnuts !

  • Forms of fried dough (doughnuts) can be traced to ancient cultures, including the Romans and Chinese.
  • Dutch Settlers introduced "olykeoks (oily cakes) to New amsterdam (now considered to be New York) in the 17th century.
